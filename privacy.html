Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how remodel-home ("we", "the app") handles information when you use our home-remodel visualization service.

Information we access

  • Photos you upload: images of your rooms that you choose to upload, so we can generate remodel renderings.
  • Pinterest data (only with your permission): if you connect your Pinterest account, we read your boards and pins (via the read-only scopes boards:read and pins:read) to use your inspiration images. We never post to Pinterest and never access data you have not authorized.
  • Account identity: the signed-in identity used to access the app (via Google Identity-Aware Proxy).

How we use your information

  • To generate AI renderings of your rooms using Google's Gemini image models.
  • To display design trends and inspiration, and to let you save and revisit generated looks.

Pinterest inspiration images

When you connect a Pinterest board, we fetch an inspiration image only at the moment it is needed, pass it to the image model to generate your rendering, and then discard it. We do not permanently store your Pinterest pin images, and we do not use them to train any model.

Third-party processing

Image generation is performed by Google's Gemini API. Your room photos and inspiration images are sent to Google solely to produce your requested rendering. We do not sell your data or share it with third parties for advertising.

Data retention

Photos you upload and renderings you generate are retained only to provide the service to you, and are deleted when you remove them or on request. Pinterest pin images are not retained beyond the moment of generation.

Your choices

You can disconnect Pinterest at any time, delete uploaded photos and generated looks, and request deletion of your data by contacting us.
